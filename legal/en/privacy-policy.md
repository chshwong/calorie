# Privacy Policy

We collect information necessary to operate the App, including:
- Account identifiers (e.g. email)
- Profile data (age, height, weight, goals)
- Logs (food, exercise, water, weight)
- App usage and interaction data
- Device and diagnostic data

---

## 2. Health-Related Data
Some information may be considered health-related.  
By using the App, you explicitly consent to its collection and processing for app functionality, personalization, progress tracking, and analytics in de-identified form.

## 3. How We Use Information
We use data to:
- Provide and improve the App
- Generate estimates and summaries

- Maintain security and reliability
- Perform aggregated analytics

---

## 4. Aggregated and De-Identified Data
We may aggregate and de-identify data and use it for analytics, research, and monetized insights.

We do not sell personally identifiable information.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

---

## 6. Data Retention
Personal data is retained while your account is active.

## 8. International Transfers
Data may be processed outside your country of residence subject to appropriate safeguards.
